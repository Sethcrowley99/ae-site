How It Works

Three steps. Two minutes to go live.

01

On the cart, a "Reveal Your Discount" button opens the game. Shoppers enter their email to play — captured into your customer list before the meter even starts.

Email Capture

Email capture that doesn't cost you margin

Popups trade a flat discount for every email. Spin wheels give a discount to 100% of players. Revelio captures emails whether shoppers win, win small, or crash to zero.

Revelio is different. Shoppers enter their email to play — and sometimes they crash. You capture the email whether they cash out with a discount, a small one, or nothing at all. Many captures happen with zero margin given up.

Every email flows straight into your Shopify customer list with marketing consent, and syncs to Klaviyo automatically. Your existing flows just pick them up — no Zapier, no CSVs, no middleware.

Because Revelio has a real crash risk, it filters out the throwaway-email problem that floods spin-wheel lists. You get a cleaner list of genuinely engaged shoppers, built at lower cost than any popup.

Quick Setup

Go live in under two minutes

No developers. No code. Email sync works out of the box.

  1. Install Revelio from the Shopify App Store.
  2. Open the Revelio dashboard in your Shopify admin. Set your max discount and crash weights.
  3. Connect Klaviyo (optional) — captured emails sync automatically alongside native Shopify customer sync.
  4. Go to Online Store → Themes → Customize. Add the Reveal My Price block to your Cart page.
  5. Hit Save. You're live.

Key configuration options

Maximum Discount
The highest percentage a shopper can potentially reach. This is the ceiling — most will cash out below it.
Recommended: 15–25%
Crash Weights
Distribute crash probability across 5% brackets to shape the experience. Higher crash rates mean more zero-margin email captures.
e.g. 40% / 30% / 20% / 10%
Email Sync
Native Shopify customer sync is on by default with marketing consent flagged. Connect Klaviyo in one click for automatic flow integration.
Shopify + Klaviyo ready
Cooldown & Volume Cap
Prevent repeat plays with a per-shopper cooldown and cap daily sessions to control total discount exposure.
Recommended: 24-hour cooldown
